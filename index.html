Inspiration

Our Inspiration

The story we grown-up with some users of sign-languages. And we learned it by heart. And it was hard in many situations to explain them everything. So, the dream kept silent?
And in a birthday of a relative. Sign-language user. He was lost his way when he coming back from work. And we tried to call him but it should be video. Then was very hard situation. Because lack of communications. Like many other hard situations, TV programs, Lectures or for story.
we believe we can provide them with all those feelings and understanding instantly through our digital model. So, we created Micro-KI to be the tool between speakers and sign-language speaks.
We are building out hub for digital artificial sign-language to help all the deafness communities and who speaks to them by personal & interpersonal language.
The idea has pretty grown up from helping our friends and relatives to help all the deafness communities around the world.
